TECHMART

Techmart, the much awaited annual Science and Technical festival of the Institute is an intellectually stimulating event that provides an opportunity for the students to interact with the experts in various fields of technology. The idea is to foster creativity and innovation in students through a suitable platform. This technical event brings out the competitive spirit of the students who are always willing to excel. Here students get a chance to display their talent in Robotics, Coding, Adwar, Movimaking, Games, Junkyard ,Pixels and many more technical events.
